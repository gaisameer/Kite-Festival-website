Sumesh Mangalasseri

Uplifting communities, one tour at a time.

Sumesh Mangalasseri, founder of Kabani tours, is a forerunner of sustainable tourism; an alternative outlook at the traditional and commercial income-generator. It was during his monotonous days in the corporate sphere, that he decided to change the irresponsible manner in which the sector was handled.
Having been raised in a background that embraced nature, he felt the crux of his venture should be no different. Developing an ingenious framework that ensures socio-economic development of local communities, Sumesh has managed to enhance tourist experience by letting them enjoy life in all its beauty, just the way it is lived around there.

Manju Sara Rajan

Expression can take many different forms.

A journalist, an editor and a CEO, our next speaker Manju Sara Rajan has taken on numerous personas in her life that most would find overwhelming. Having practiced journalism for 15 years, as a reporter for the Time magazine in Hong Kong, a correspondent for the Delhi bureau of International News magazine, founding editor of the Architectural Digest, India and serving as an editor for various journals and magazines, she has made a name for herself in both national and international media.
In 2016 she took over as the first female CEO of the Kochin Muziris Biennale and pulled off one of the world’s largest exhibitions of contemporary art that showcased 98 artists across 12 venues. From her varied experience, she is of the opinion that it is not necessary to understand art but accepting that expression can take so many different forms is vital.
A charismatic and influential figure, Manju Sara Rajan stands as a symbol of women empowerment as showcased by this Millennium.
Witness this icon speak her mind at TEDxCET 2019.

SREEJESH NAIR

Amplifying the emotion in sound.

Sreejesh Nair, an acclaimed sound artist and technology evangelist, boasts a filmography with over 200 movies, that have garnered a global audience. A national award winner, he has delivered exceptional content for some of the biggest international production houses that include Warner Bros, Sony Pictures, Disney and Paramount.
Experimenting with dynamic new techniques that seamlessly blend aural and visual content, Sreejesh has managed to craft designs that are rich in a spectrum of sentiments. Nicknamed ‘Flying fingers’, his swift, yet efficient work ethic has helped him raise his game over the years and inspire those around him.
Be all ears as he delves into a whole new dimension of hearing, at TEDxCET 2019.

ABOUT H2O

Helping Hands Organisation (H2O) is a not-for-profit organisation that focuses on supporting children with Autism and other learning and behavioural differences and is based out of Trivandrum, Kerala. However, while rehabilitation is our primary focus, our vision is larger – “To create a better world for the deprived and the needy giving special focus to differently abled children.Registered under the Travancore-Cochin Literary, Scientific; charitable societies Registration Act XII of 1955 vide Reg. No. TVM/TC/1589/2012, H2O was established in 2012, and has since achieved 80G and 12A approvals from income tax department. In our tryst to help with as many issues as possible, we have been able to lend our hands to elder care, underprivileged children and have also been able to concentrate on educating young minds in actively participating and addressing civic issues around them, in collaboration with CMCA. We have a volunteer base of over 1500 youths, mostly college students, in Thiruvananthapuram alone, and are currently increasing our volunteer base across the state.”
