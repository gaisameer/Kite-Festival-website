Kites For Freedom - How BFIKF Came To Be

The BFIKF 2018, was H2O's initiative to involve our diffabled beneficiaries in kite flying, along with the myriad beach-goers to break the barriers. Slowly, but surely, we had more diffabled participants join us, and even more donors and patrons, supporting this one-of-a-kind event. And with this enthusiasm, H2O was successful in conducting India's first ever inclusive and Barrier Free Kite Fest, with over 300 differently abled participants, 30 National and International Kite Flyers, and 10,000 non-diffabled participants and viewers present at the Kovalam Beach. Together, we truly broke barriers. Together, we celebrated inclusivity. And together, we are back again for BFIKF 2019!
